Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a small group of passionate developers with a vision to create a collaborative space for the tech community. What started as a small, local meetup with 50 attendees has grown into a major international conference. Our commitment to accessibility and cutting-edge topics has made TechCon a must-attend event for professionals and students alike.

>

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a global community of innovators, providing a platform for the exchange of ideas and the exploration of new technologies. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment where everyone, from seasoned veterans to new developers, can learn, grow, and be inspired.

Meet Our Past Speakers

Dr. Anya Sharma

A pioneer in quantum computing, Dr. Sharma's research has led to groundbreaking advancements in the field. She spoke at TechCon 2022 on the topic of "Quantum Computing for Everyone," demystifying complex principles and inspiring a new generation of researchers.
